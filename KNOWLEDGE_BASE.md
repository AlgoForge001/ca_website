## 5. Required Documents (Checklists)

### ITR Filing (FY 2025-26 / AY 2026-27)
1. PAN Card
2. Aadhaar Card
3. Bank Account Details (with IFSC)
4. Form 16 / Salary Slips
5. Form 26AS & AIS / TIS
6. Interest Certificates (Bank, FD, Post Office)
7. Investment Proofs (LIC, PPF, ELSS, NPS, etc.)
8. Home Loan Interest Certificate
9. Capital Gain Statements (Shares, MF, Property)
10. Rent Receipts / HRA Proofs
11. Medical Insurance Premium Receipts
12. Donation Receipts (80G)

### GST Registration
1. PAN Card of Proprietor / Firm / Company
2. Aadhaar Card of Proprietor / Partners / Directors
3. Passport Size Photograph
4. Business Address Proof (Electricity Bill / Property Tax Receipt / Rent Agreement)
5. If Rented Property – NOC from Owner
6. Bank Account Proof (Cancelled Cheque / Bank Statement / Passbook)
7. Business Registration Proof (Partnership Deed / COI / Shop Act / MSME)
8. Mobile Number & Email ID
9. Digital Signature (for Company / LLP)

### Udyam (MSME) Registration
1. Aadhaar Card of Proprietor / Partner / Director
2. PAN Card of Business / Proprietor
3. Mobile Number linked with Aadhaar & Email ID
4. Business Address Details
5. Bank Account Details (Account Number & IFSC Code)
6. Type of Business Activity (Manufacturing / Service / Trading)
7. Number of Employees
8. Investment in Plant & Machinery / Equipment
9. Annual Turnover Details
10. GST Number (if available)

### Food Licence (FSSAI Registration)
1. Aadhaar Card of Proprietor / Partner / Director
2. PAN Card of Proprietor / Firm / Company
3. Passport Size Photograph
4. Business Address Proof (Electricity Bill / Rent Agreement / Property Tax Receipt)
5. Shop & Establishment / Trade License (if applicable)
6. Nature of Food Business Details
7. List of Food Products
8. Bank Account Proof
9. Mobile Number & Email ID
10. NOC from Owner (if rented)

### Partnership Deed Preparation
1. PAN & Aadhaar of All Partners
2. Passport Size Photographs of All Partners
3. Mobile Number & Email ID of All Partners
4. Business Name Proposed & Nature of Activity
5. Business Address Proof & NOC from Owner
6. Capital Contribution Details of Each Partner
7. Profit & Loss Sharing Ratio
8. Authorized Partner Details
9. Witness ID Proof
